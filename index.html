Proven Patterns

7 patterns from live campaigns.

Each one confirmed across 3+ data points. Not best practices. Evidence.

P001

Trust Bridge

The free audit converts at 85% once someone replies. The audit is the product demo. Never sell before it.

P002

Hidden Differentiator

Every company audited had at least one strong proof point buried where buyers can't see it. Finding it builds instant trust.

P003

Score Hook

"Scored [Company] out of 100" in the subject line. 6.7% reply rate with consulting founders. Curiosity without clickbait.

P004

2-Hour Window

6 of 9 replies that converted to audit requests came within 2 hours. Speed of delivery matters more than perfection.

P005

JS Rendering Gap

3 of 5 Wix sites scored zero on technical SEO. Google can't read JavaScript-rendered content. Instant finding for any audit.

P006

Service Menu Overload

Every company listed too many services. The fix: 3 pillars, 3 pages, 3 entry points. Buyer self-selects in 10 seconds.

P007

Signal Over Volume

300 signal-qualified leads outperformed 900 cold ones. The hiring signal or funding round proves timing. Cold lists prove nothing.
